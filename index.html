Privacy Policy — BrawnHeart Auto-Post

Last updated: 2026-05-04

This Privacy Policy describes how the BrawnHeart Auto-Post application ("the App") handles information.

1. Operator

The App is operated by an individual page administrator for personal use on their own Facebook Pages. There is no public service offered to other users.

Contact: nedc.tw@gmail.com

2. Information We Access

To publish posts to the operator's own Facebook Pages, the App accesses the following via the Meta Graph API:

  • Page Access Token for Pages where the operator is admin
  • Page ID of those Pages
  • Post insights (impressions, reach, engagement) for the operator's own posts, used for internal performance tracking only

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or process any data from end users (Page visitors, followers, or commenters). The App only publishes content authored by the operator.

3. How We Use Information

  • Page Access Tokens are used solely to call the Graph API endpoint that publishes posts to the operator's own Pages.
  • Page IDs identify which Page receives the post.
  • Insights are read for the operator's review only.

No information is shared with third parties.
No information is used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than the operator's own publishing workflow.

4. Storage and Security

  • Tokens are stored locally on the operator's machine and on a private Cloudflare Worker secret store, with TLS in transit.
  • Tokens are never transmitted to any third party.
  • The App runs on infrastructure controlled by the operator (their own machine, GitHub Actions, Cloudflare Workers).

5. Retention

Tokens are retained until the operator revokes them via Facebook account settings or removes them from local credentials files. Posts published by the App remain on Facebook subject to Facebook's own retention.

6. User Rights

Because the App does not process data from any end user, there are no end-user rights requests to handle. The Page admin can:

  • Revoke the App's access to their Pages at any time via Facebook Account > Apps and Websites
  • Delete posts published by the App via Facebook's standard interface
  • Delete local credentials files at any time

7. Data Deletion

To request deletion of any data the App may hold:

  • Email: nedc.tw@gmail.com
  • Or: revoke the App from Facebook Account > Settings > Apps and Websites, which immediately invalidates all tokens.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated. The "Last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision.
